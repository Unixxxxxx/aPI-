Day 1: Haridwar → Guptkashi

• Start early morning from Haridwar/Rishikesh.

• Drive through Devprayag, Rudraprayag & beautiful Himalayan valleys.

• Check-in at Guptkashi. Evening visit to Ardhnarishwar Temple.

Day 2: Guptkashi → Gaurikund → Kedarnath Trek

• Drive to Gaurikund (starting point of trek).

• Begin the 16 km trek to Kedarnath. Options: walking, pony, or palki.

• Reach Kedarnath by evening. Check into camps/lodges.

Day 3: Kedarnath Darshan → Return to Guptkashi

• Early morning darshan at Kedarnath Temple.

• Explore Bhairavnath Temple.

• Trek back to Gaurikund and return to Guptkashi.

Day 4: Guptkashi → Rishikesh/Haridwar

• Drive back through picturesque Himalayan towns.

• Drop at Rishikesh or Haridwar. Trip ends with blessings of Mahadev.
